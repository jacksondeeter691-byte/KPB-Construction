Privacy Policy

How KPB Construction LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 1, 2026  ·  Effective: April 1, 2026

Summary: We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to request a quote or contact us. We do not sell your data. We use Tawk.to live chat and Google Fonts, which may set cookies. You can opt out of non-essential cookies at any time.

1. Who We Are

KPB Construction LLC ("KPB," "we," "us," or "our") is a family-owned handyman and construction company located at 207 Short Street, Frankton, Indiana 46044. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at:

  • Phone: (765) 881-2658
  • Email: info@kpbconstruction.com
  • Address: 207 Short Street, Frankton, IN 46044

2. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it to us, or when it is automatically collected as part of normal website operation.

Information you provide:

  • Contact/Estimate Form: First name, last name, phone number, email address (optional), service type, and project description.
  • Live Chat (Tawk.to): Any messages you send through the chat widget on our website.
  • Direct contact (phone/email): Information you share when calling or emailing us directly.

Information collected automatically:

  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device by third-party services (see Section 5). We do not set first-party cookies.
  • Log data: Your web browser may transmit standard log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) to our hosting provider.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your estimate requests and inquiries.
  • Schedule site visits and project consultations.
  • Communicate about your project (quotes, timelines, updates).
  • Follow up after project completion.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing automation, or any purpose unrelated to fulfilling your service request.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party platforms we use to operate the website (Tawk.to, Google Fonts — described in Section 5). These providers have their own privacy policies and are contractually limited to using your data only for the services they provide.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or court order, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations.
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a sale or merger of KPB Construction LLC, customer data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via this page.

5. Cookies and Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may set cookies or collect data:

Tawk.to (Live Chat)

  • Purpose: Powers the live chat and messaging feature on our website.
  • Data collected: Chat messages, approximate geographic location (city-level), browser/device information, pages visited during your session.
  • Cookies set: Tawk.to sets session and analytics cookies.
  • Privacy policy: tawk.to/privacy-policy
  • Opt-out: You can decline cookies via our cookie consent banner and the live chat will not load.

Google Fonts

  • Purpose: Loads the DM Sans and Playfair Display typefaces used on this website.
  • Data collected: Google may log your IP address when fonts are requested. No personally identifiable information is associated with font requests.
  • Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner is displayed. You may:

  • Accept Cookies: Allows Tawk.to and associated cookies to load, enabling the live chat feature.
  • Decline: Prevents Tawk.to from loading. The website remains fully functional without the live chat.

Your preference is saved in your browser's localStorage. To reset your choice, clear your browser's local storage or site data and revisit the page.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Estimate requests: Kept for the duration of your project engagement, and for up to 2 years afterward for warranty and follow-up purposes.
  • Chat logs: Retained within Tawk.to's platform per their data retention policy. Contact Tawk.to directly for deletion requests related to chat data.
  • Email/phone inquiries: Retained as long as reasonably needed for business records, typically no more than 3 years.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your information ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to opt out: You may opt out of any non-essential data collection at any time by declining cookies via the banner or contacting us directly.
  • California residents (CCPA): California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (765) 881-2658 or by email at info@kpbconstruction.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our website, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Thumbtack, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
