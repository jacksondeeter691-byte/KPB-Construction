FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you might want to know before reaching out.

How do I get a quote? +
Just give us a call or fill out the contact form below. We'll schedule a time to look at your project and provide a free, no-obligation estimate — usually within 1–2 business days.
What areas do you serve? +
We primarily serve Carmel, IN and surrounding communities including Westfield, Fishers, Noblesville, Zionsville, and parts of Indianapolis. Not sure if we cover your area? Just ask!
Are you background checked and insured? +
Yes — we are background checked. We take your safety and trust very seriously, and we're happy to provide documentation upon request.
What size projects do you take on? +
We work on everything from small handyman tasks (furniture assembly, fixture swaps, minor repairs) to larger projects like bathroom updates, deck repairs, and interior remodeling. If you're not sure whether your project is a fit, just reach out!
How quickly can you get started? +
It depends on current availability, but we do our best to accommodate urgent requests. After your estimate is approved, we'll schedule your project as quickly as possible.
Do you offer any guarantees? +
Absolutely. Your satisfaction is our #1 priority. If something isn't right, we'll make it right. We stand behind our work and want every customer to love their results.
